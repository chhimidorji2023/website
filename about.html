About Royal Academy Bhutan

A place where aesthetics meets technology

Our Vision

The Royal Academy Bhutan (DGI) aims to nurture students who are creative, curious, and compassionate. By combining aesthetics and technology, the Academy helps learners explore both artistic expression and modern innovation.

What We Do

We offer a holistic education that encourages balance between the mind, body, and spirit. Students engage in diverse forms of learning such as arts, science, physical education, and moral development — guided by Bhutan’s unique philosophy of Gross National Happiness.

Learning Approach

The Academy’s approach blends traditional Bhutanese values with global perspectives. Students participate in hands-on projects, creative performances, and collaborative learning that prepare them for future challenges while staying rooted in their culture.

Location

Located amidst the serene hills of Pangbisa, Paro, the campus provides a peaceful and inspiring environment for learning and self-discovery.
